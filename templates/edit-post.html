Ibyibutswa

Gutanga ikibazo ni ubuntu. Dore bimwe mu bishobora kugufasha:

  • Niba ufite igihe gihagije, tubwire byinshi ku kibazo cyawe tugufashe
  • Niba udafite igihe kinini, ba utubajije ibishoboka, kuko nyuma yaho ushobora kugira ibyo wongera mu kibazo.
  • Mu gihe ikibazo cyawe gishobora gufasha abandi, kandi akaba atari ik'ibanga ushobora kwemera ko abandi bifatanya, cyangwa kikaba rusange, bigatuma dushakira umuti hamwe.
